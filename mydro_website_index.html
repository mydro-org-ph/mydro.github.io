About MYDRO

The Monkayo Youth Disaster Response Organization (MYDRO) is a youth-led initiative committed to strengthening community resilience, enhancing disaster preparedness, and empowering the youth of Monkayo through proactive leadership and coordinated emergency response actions. Guided by the spirit of service, MYDRO works closely with local partners to ensure that every disaster‑related effort is informed, organized, and community-centered.

Programs & Operations

Disaster Preparedness Training

MYDRO conducts workshops, orientations, and drills to equip youth responders and community members with the knowledge and skills needed to act during emergencies.

Community Risk Assessment

We assist in profiling hazard-prone areas and creating youth-led risk maps to support barangay disaster planning.

Emergency Communications

Our PR and Communications Unit ensures timely dissemination of safety advisories and information before, during, and after disasters.

Disaster Response

MYDRO mobilizes trained youth responders to assist in rapid assessments, relief operations, and coordinated emergency support in partnership with local agencies.

Organizational Structure

The organization follows a structured yet dynamic leadership model composed of:

  • Executive Committee (Chairperson, Vice Chairpersons)
  • Operations & Response Unit
  • Public Relations & Communications Unit
  • Logistics & Resource Management Unit
  • Training & Capacity Building Unit

Volunteer With Us

Young people in Monkayo are welcome to join MYDRO as volunteers and responders. Sign up and become part of our growing network of youth supporting safe, resilient communities.
